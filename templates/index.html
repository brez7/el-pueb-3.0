Catering Summary

Our catering is priced per person. You get to choose which types of meat you want. For $18 per person, you get:

  • Choice of two meats

    • The meat choices include Carne Asada, Pollo Asado (Chicken) and Adobada (Marinated Pork). You can combine them or select once choice. You also have the option to add an extra meat choice for an additional cost.

  • Guacamole
  • Pico de Gallo
  • Rice and Beans
  • Red Salsa (Mild)
  • Green Salsa (Medium)
  • Orange Salsa (Hot)

This is the basic catering package. You can also select the following items at an additional cost:

  • Third Meat Option
  • Quesadillas
  • Shrimp
  • Sour Cream
  • Bunuelos
